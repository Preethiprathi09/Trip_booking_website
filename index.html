Tour To Coorg Today!

Coorg(Kodagu) is a premier travel destination where heritage and nature are inextricably linked, offering a trip that balances deep cultural immersion with ecological exploration. Its importance for trip details lies in providing diverse experiences ranging from spiritual retreats in historic temples to high-energy adventure in its biodiversity hotspots.

POPULAR DESTINATIONS

Golden Temple

The Namdroling Monastery, popularly known as the Golden Temple, is a major Tibetan Buddhist center located in Bylakuppe, near Kushalnagar in Kodagu (Coorg), Karnataka. Established in 1963, it features stunning, 60-foot golden statues of Buddha, intricate Tibetan architecture, and serene gardens its a peacefull place.

Abbi falls

About 9 km from Madikeri is the beautiful sight of an impetuous mountain stream cascading over a rockface. The refreshingly cool waters of Abbey Falls, and the greenery of the surrounding coffee and cardamom estates make it a very popular picnic spot.

Read More
Talakaveri

Talakaveri, located on the Brahmagiri hills in Kodagu, is the revered birthplace of the sacred Kaveri River. Situated at 1,276 meters, this holy pilgrimage site features a temple dedicated to Goddess Kaveriamma and a sacred tank 46 km from Madikeri.

Read More
Dubare

The three-hour elephant interaction is destined to be the most unforgettable experience at Dubare camp. It kicks off with a 45 minute bath in the Cauvery, where you scrub an elephant and groom with oil from close proximity. You can also participate in feeding the elephants with ragi, jaggery, sugarcane, banana and coconuts.
